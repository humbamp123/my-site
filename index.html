I'm a Software Engineer with an unconventional path—bridging Environmental Resources Engineering with modern software development.

My time at Humboldt State, surrounded by ancient redwoods and wild rivers, taught me to think in systems. Whether it's water flow dynamics or data pipelines, the principles remain: understand the whole, optimize for sustainability, build for resilience.

Currently at Qlik/Stitch Data, I specialize in building robust data systems and full-stack applications. I'm also a graduate of 42 School, where I sharpened my skills in C programming and algorithmic thinking.
